Workflow.

I divide work into stages with approval at each step

01

Briefing

We meet and discuss project goals and objectives. I fully immerse myself in your business processes and generate the best ideas.

02

Analytics

Serious preparation stage. I research your competitors, evaluate important details, find strengths and weaknesses to use in website creation.

03

Prototyping

Creating a prototype - the "skeleton" of the future website with full text content and layout. This stage determines block sequence and customer journey.

04

Design

My favorite part! Creating graphic elements, selecting content, fonts, and color palette for the project design.

05

Development

Responsive design adaptation for all screen sizes and mobile devices. Remember: mobile traffic is about 80% of all users!

06

Launch Prep

Connecting domain, setting up SSL certificate, SEO optimization, connecting analytics and metrics.

07

Contract

Drafting and signing the contract by all parties.

08

Launch!

Your website goes live! Continuous support and updates as needed.
